How to Read This as an Owner

The pilot is not about abstract technology. It is about one concrete question: “Does this layer of memory and messaging grow House of Wines’ business enough to justify keeping it on?”

If total reservations, estimated revenue, repeat guests, and upsell acceptance are meaningfully better with Graceful than without it, the answer reveals itself.

This dashboard is maintained by Murray Company Limited for House of Wines as part of the Graceful Concierge 30-day pilot. It is not intended for public guests — only for ownership and trusted staff.
