Graceful Concierge collects phone numbers only from customers who voluntarily provide their number through our website forms, booking requests, or direct communication with our team.

By submitting a phone number, you explicitly consent to receive SMS messages related to:

  • Appointment confirmations
  • Service updates
  • Customer support communication
  • Responses to your direct inquiries

Message & data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on service activity.

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP. For assistance, reply HELP or email graceful@murraycompanylimited.com.

We do not sell or share mobile numbers for marketing purposes and do not use purchased lists.
