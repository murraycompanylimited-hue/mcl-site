Murray Company Limited · Mobile Food Division

Phresh Global

A hydrogen-powered mobile food network built to feed communities, reduce waste, and deliver fast, healthy meals at scale — with zero emissions. Designed for rapid deployment, festival circuits, disaster relief, and daily local service.

Capabilities

Phresh units operate like mobile micro-kitchens with high-efficiency output.

  • Hydrogen fuel cell mobile kitchens
  • Farm-to-cart integrated supply model
  • Rapid deployment for events, disasters, and pop-up zones
  • Subscription meal networks for cities & campuses
  • Zero-emission cooking & prep environments

Partnerships & Licensing

Phresh Global is open to franchise-style licensing, municipal partnerships, and national event routing. All deals run through Murray Company Limited under private contract.
