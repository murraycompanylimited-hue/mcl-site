Donnie Dictionary

A focused lexicon lab decoding language, systems, and paperwork. One signal word at a time, without the fear, hype, or filler that usually comes with this territory.

What this lane is for

Donnie Dictionary exists to strip the fog off words that matter in real life: banking, paperwork, contracts, “citizen,” “person,” status, identity, and all the gray-area terms people throw around without understanding.

It’s not about impressing anyone with Latin. It’s about translating the words that control people’s lives into clean, usable understanding you can actually act on.

Current focus

  • Short, direct breakdowns of high-friction words and phrases.
  • Connecting terms across law, finance, trust, and everyday life.
  • Creating a consistent language backbone for the rest of the MCL ecosystem.

How to engage

If you want a word decoded, don’t send an essay. Pick one or two terms that matter to you, tell me where you saw them (document, form, conversation), and what you’re trying to do with them. That’s enough to start.

Subject line suggestion (for email): “Donnie Dictionary – word request: [YOUR WORD HERE]”
