Clan of Murray Human Alliance

A membership association focused on clean, aligned commerce and lawful value flow, built for people who want to work together on purpose – not in chaos.

A nonprofit-focused lane for feeding, housing, and supporting humans under a simple rule: if it were you, how would you want to be treated?

What this lane is for

This is the impact arm – the place where time, energy, and money are directed toward feeding people, keeping them sheltered, and supporting those in real medical and life hardship.

It is not a branding exercise. It’s the part of the ecosystem built to show up where things are rough, quietly and consistently.

Current focus

  • Clarifying pilot projects around food, shelter, and direct support.
  • Aligning nonprofit paperwork and language with the Golden Rule.
  • Building relationships with people and places where help actually lands.

How to engage

If you’re reaching out about this lane, say which side you are on: needing help, wanting to help, or wanting to collaborate on a specific project.
