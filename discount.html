What this lane is for

Donnie Discount is the “get it done” channel – calling vendors, finding gear, solving supply issues, and doing the work that happens before a sale ever shows up on a spreadsheet.

This isn’t coupon-hunting. It’s understanding where cost, quality, and timing meet in the real world.

Current focus

  • Helping people and small operations source what they actually need, not whatever’s being pushed.
  • Finding practical ways to save money without wrecking quality or relationships.
  • Documenting sourcing wins that plug back into other MCL lanes.

How to engage

If you reach out about Donnie Discount, say what you need, what you’re paying now (if anything), and what “better” means to you (cheaper, faster, higher quality, more reliable).
